Meet Our Chef's

Executive Chef

With over 20 years of experience in the culinary world, Chef John Doe has become known for his innovative takes on classic dishes. He

attended culinary school at the prestigious Le Cordon Bleu in Paris and has worked in some of the world's most renowned kitchens, including Noma in Copenhagen and The French Laundry in Napa Valley. Chef Doe's philosophy on food is simple: use the freshest, most seasonal ingredients possible and let the quality of those ingredients shine through in every dish. Some of his signature dishes include a roasted beet salad with goat cheese and pistachios, and a slow-braised beef short rib with a red wine reduction.

Chef De Cuisine

Chef Jane Smith grew up in the Pacific Northwest and has always had a passion for local and sustainable ingredients. After

attending culinary school in Portland, she worked her way up through the ranks at some of the city's top restaurants before opening her own restaurant, Smith's Kitchen, in 2015. Chef Smith is committed to using only the freshest, most seasonal ingredients in her cooking, and she sources many of her ingredients from local farmers and producers. Some of her signature dishes include a Pacific Northwest seafood chowder with bacon and cornbread, and a roasted butternut squash with sage and brown butter.

Head Chef

Chef Alex Rodriguez grew up in a family of chefs and restaurateurs and has been cooking since he was a young child. He attended

culinary school in Mexico City before moving to the United States to work in some of New York City's top kitchens. Chef Rodriguez is known for his creative takes on Mexican cuisine, and he combines traditional Mexican flavors with modern techniques and ingredients. Some of his signature dishes include a grilled skirt steak with a chimichurri sauce and a jalapeño-lime salsa, and a hibiscus flower margarita with a smoked salt rim.
